Matrix Bot — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

1. Introduction

Matrix Bot (“we”, “our”, or “the bot”) is a Discord application designed to help communities manage esports tournaments, scrims, and roles. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any data collected during normal bot operation.

2. Information We Collect

Matrix Bot collects only the minimum information required to perform its tournament and moderation functions within Discord. This may include:

  • Discord user IDs (for team registration, role assignment, and group/channel permissions)
  • Guild (server) IDs and channel IDs (to store tournament data and settings)
  • Team names, slot numbers, and tournament configuration details

Matrix Bot does NOT collect or store: emails, passwords, payment information, or other personal identifying information (PII).

3. How We Use the Data

We use collected data only within Discord to:

  • Manage tournaments, scrims, and team registrations
  • Assign or remove roles automatically
  • Create private group channels and manage permissions
  • Generate points tables and graphics for display in Discord

We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or tracking purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

Tournament and configuration data is stored on the bot’s hosting server. Data access is restricted to authorized administrators. We implement reasonable security measures to protect stored data. While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Tournament data (registered teams and settings) is retained only while the tournament is active. After completion or deletion of an event, related data may be removed automatically. Server owners can also request a full data wipe via the bot’s management commands or by contacting support.

6. Third-Party Services

Matrix Bot interacts only with Discord’s official API. We do not use external analytics or advertising services that collect user information outside of Discord.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access or deletion of your data
  • Remove the bot from your server at any time
  • Contact us for clarification about data handling
